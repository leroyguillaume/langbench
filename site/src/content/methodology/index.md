This section explains what `langbench` measures, what it refuses to measure, and why.
A benchmark whose methodology is not published is worth nothing, so these pages are a
deliverable, not a footnote.

They describe the rules the harness is written against: what a workload has to be, how
a backend declares itself, what is timed and what is only prepared, which statistic is
allowed to be quoted, and which claims are not on offer at any resolution.

If you are here to dispute a number, this is the right place. Start with
[What is under test](what-is-under-test/), and in particular with
[Claims we do not make](what-is-under-test/#claims-we-do-not-make).
